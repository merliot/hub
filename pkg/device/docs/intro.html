Merliot Hub is your private device hub, allowing you to monitor and control your devices over the Internet, privately.

WHAT DEVICES?

Well, sorry, not the smart devices you buy at the big box store. Those aren't private! Merliot Hub requires you to build your devices using Raspberry Pis, microcontrollers, and parts you can readily buy online.

The hub has instructions and parts lists to build each device. You download the firmware for the device from the hub, so there is no programming required. Once installed, the device will securely connect to the hub.

Deploy your hub on the cloud or on your server. View your hub and devices from any web browser, even on your phone. There is no app to install.

Try the demo to see examples of supported device models.

SUPPORTED DEVICE TARGETS

Raspberry Pi
Arduino Nano RP2040 Connect
Seed Wio Terminal
Adafruit PyPortal
Linux x86-64
Koyeb (Cloud)

Merliot is written in Go, TinyGo, and htmx. Thank you to those who built and maintain these fine tools.
